USGS NWIS Instantaneous Values API — waterservices.usgs.gov/nws/iv/ — real-time gauge data in JSON/WaterML, updated every 15 minutes. This is your core data feed.

NWS API — api.weather.gov — forecast data, alerts, and river stage forecasts.

NOAA AHPS — water.noaa.gov — river forecasts with predicted crests and flood stage thresholds.

USACE Chicago District — the Army Corps publishes lock and dam operational data, though it's less consistently API-accessible than USGS.

Iowa Environmental Mesonet — mesonet.agron.iastate.edu — aggregates a ton of Midwest hydrology and weather data with good APIs.

NOAA MRMS — precipitation QPE via NOAA's NOMADS server.
